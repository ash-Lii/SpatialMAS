# SpatialMAS Example Research Questions

1. Which geographic zones show the highest concentration of events during peak hours?
2. How does demand differ by weekday vs weekend across service regions?
3. Which entities have the largest month-over-month growth in activity?
4. Are there strong correlations between travel time and route distance by area?
5. Which categories contribute most to long-tail outliers in response time?
6. How do incident hotspots shift over time windows (hour/day/month)?
